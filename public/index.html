The Model

3D Model (Build a Base)

I was tasked with creating a 3D model with the prompt "Build a Base", I chose to model my room on this prompt. I chose my room, as it contains a lot of different objects with complex shapes, which will allow me to demonstrate a range of skill within 3D modelling. Blender is my software of choice when modelling, while i am very familiar with 3Ds Max, I find Blender more intuitive and powerful, especially when rendering within software, rather than exporting my models to unity.

Mechanic (Illusions)

My next task was to create a mechanic with the prompt "Illusions". I was inspired by the T.A.R.D.I.S from Doctor Who, to create a bigger on the inside effect. I did some research and found a Brackeys https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=cuQao3hEKfs tutorial on a similar portal effect and decided to use it as a base. I created a quick T.A.R.D.I.S exterior model and found a simple free interior model on TurboSquid, and imported them into unity, neither of them looked perfect but they looked good enough for a prototype mechanic/proof of concept

The Narrative

I was tasked with writing a narrative based on the prompt of "Lively Crowd". My first thought when hearing the prompt was of a bar, so I decided to set the narrative in a bar.

Narrative writing (Lively Crowd)

I walk in and get assaulted by the, smell, and the noise, and the people. God I hate this place... But she told me to meet her here, so what choice do i have. I see her sitting at the bar, wearing that stupid jacket, sticking out like a sore thumb. I walk up to her, and say "hello sweetie" she says hello back. I order a drink, before we head outside to the smoking area. "Those things are gonna kill you one day sweetheart" I already know that. The music is still so loud, even muffled by the walls, at least the smoke drowns out the smell, and the wind keeps the air cool.
